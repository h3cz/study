Class Study Lab

Every group builds a small original bank, reviews another group's work, imports it in local development or an enabled fork, and improves explanations after a practice run.

  1. Setup the app.
  2. Pick one objective or lecture topic.
  3. Create 5 questions, 3 flashcards, and 1 matching drill idea.
  4. Peer-review source hygiene and clarity.
  5. Import, quiz, and revise.
