# Peer Review Checklist

Review a classmate's bank with this pass:

- The stem asks one clear thing.
- There is exactly one best answer.
- Distractors are plausible but not misleading.
- The explanation says why the right answer wins.
- The explanation says enough to learn from a miss.
- The objective mapping looks correct.
- The source note is present.
- The item does not appear copied from a dump or paid bank.

Leave one concrete edit suggestion per confusing item.
